Physical Activities You should do


1. Walking: Releases endorphins, improves mood.

2. Stretching: Relaxes muscles, increases flexibility.

3. Jogging or Running: Releases endorphins, improves cardiovascular health.

4.Aerobics or Zumba: Energetic, social, and improves cardiovascular health."

5. Dancing: Expressive, fun, and improves coordination.

6. Gardening: Nurtures growth, promotes relaxation..

7. Walking or Jogging with a Pet: Social, improves mood.

8. High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT): Improves cardiovascular health, boosts mood

Tips


1. Start small, gradually increase intensity and duration.

2. Find activities that bring joy and relaxation.

3. Schedule regular exercise to maintain consistency.

4. Focus on progress, not perfection.

5. Celebrate small victories to boost self-esteem.
